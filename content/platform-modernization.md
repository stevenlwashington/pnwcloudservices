## Our Approach
We follow a predictable, low-risk modernization approach:

### **1. Discover**
- Map current systems, data, workflows, and dependencies  
- Identify friction points for engineering, GTM, and operations  
- Define modernization goals tied to revenue, velocity, and cost  

### **2. Design**
- Create future-state architecture  
- Map data models and integration patterns  
- Align UX and workflows with business needs  

### **3. Migrate**
- Execute architecture changes in iterative, low-risk phases  
- Modernize services, APIs, and pipelines  
- Improve platform reliability and security  

### **4. Optimize**
- Tune performance, costs, AI readiness, and team workflows  
- Add observability and measurement  
- Validate business outcomes
